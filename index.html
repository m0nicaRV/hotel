Enjoy the tranquility of Hotel Logroño

Hotel Logroño has a central and quiet location, next to San Miguel Park. The city center of Logroño, with its historic cathedral and ancient Revellín Wall, is just a 20-minute walk away. Logroño is a city known for its Rioja wine and tapas. Going for tapas is almost a must, and the perfect area is the restaurants and bars on Laurel and San Juan streets.

The hotel's location is simply ideal:

  1. Just a five-minute drive from the Riojaforum Conference Center
  2. There is a bus stop in front of the hotel
  3. Located in the quiet area of San Miguel Park
  4. We have 108 Standard rooms, as well as two Junior Suites on the 5th floor

All rooms are very bright thanks to natural light. Half of the rooms have views of the park and the mountains.

At Hotel Logroño, we serve a hearty and varied buffet breakfast every morning in our bright breakfast room. The hotel's Open Bar is open 24 hours a day. Or if you prefer, you can enjoy our spacious lobby and lounge area with views of the park through the windows.
